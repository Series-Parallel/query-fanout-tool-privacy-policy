Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Query Fanout Chrome extension ("the Extension", "we", "our") handles user data. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Summary: Query Fanout processes data entirely on your device. We do not collect, transmit, store, or share any of your personal data with external servers or third parties.

1. Information We Access

The Extension accesses the following types of data solely to provide its core functionality:

  • Web Page Content: When you visit supported AI platforms (ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity), the Extension reads page content to extract search queries that the AI used to answer your questions.
  • API Responses: The Extension intercepts network responses from the supported platforms to identify search queries embedded in the AI's response data.

2. How We Use Information

The data accessed by the Extension is used exclusively to:

  • Display the search queries that AI models used to generate their responses
  • Present this information to you in the Extension's popup interface

This is the sole user-facing feature of the Extension, as prominently described in the Chrome Web Store listing and the Extension's user interface.

3. Data Storage

All data processing occurs locally on your device:

  • Extracted query data is temporarily stored in your browser's session storage
  • This data is automatically cleared when you close the browser tab
  • No data is saved to your device's permanent storage
  • No data is transmitted to any external servers

4. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or transfer your data to any third parties. Specifically:

  • No data is sent to our servers (we do not operate any servers)
  • No data is shared with advertisers or analytics services
  • No data is sold or monetized in any way
  • No data is transferred to third parties for any purpose

5. Data Security

Since all data remains on your device and is never transmitted externally:

  • Your data is protected by your browser's built-in security measures
  • Session storage data is isolated per-origin and per-tab
  • No network transmission means no risk of data interception

6. Permissions Used

The Extension requests only the minimum permissions necessary:

  • activeTab: To access the content of the current tab when you click the Extension icon
  • scripting: To inject scripts that read search query data from AI platforms
  • storage: For internal Extension functionality (not for storing user data)
  • Host permissions for chatgpt.com, claude.ai, perplexity.ai: To access these specific sites where the Extension provides its functionality

7. User Control

You have full control over your data:

  • Close the browser tab to clear all temporarily stored query data
  • Uninstall the Extension at any time to remove all Extension functionality
  • The Extension does not collect any data when you're not on supported AI platforms

8. Children's Privacy

The Extension is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or the Extension's data practices, please contact us:
